[00:00 – 00:30] THE HOOK & THE AI REALITY CHECK

But here’s the scary part: Most of these agents have zero guardrails. According to JailbreakBench, over 80% of undefended models can be compromised. One prompt injection is all it takes to turn your customer-facing AI into a data exfiltration tool. This is why we built Rectitude.AI."

[00:30 – 01:00] THE CONCEPT: THE 5-LAYER SECURITY SANDWICH
"Rectitude.AI isn’t just a simple classifier—it’s a multi-layered security gateway. We call it our '5-Layer Security Sandwich.' It acts as a zero-trust firewall between your users and your intelligence.

Before a prompt even reaches the LLM, we analyze intent. While the model processes, we verify capability. And before the response reaches the user, we redact sensitive data. It’s Defense-in-Depth for the generative era."

[01:00 – 02:00] CORE DEFENSE: LAYERS 1 & 2
"Let’s look at the first two lines of defense. 

Layer 1 is Intent Security. We combine a high-speed regex pre-filter with a DeBERTa-v3 transformer classifier. We aren't just looking for keywords; we're analyzing the semantic intent of attacks like role-switching or persona hijacking.

Layer 2 is where we truly differentiate: Cryptographic Integrity. Most systems trust the LLM's output. We don’t. We issue HMAC-signed capability tokens. Even if an agent is jailbroken, it physically cannot access a tool unless it has the cryptographic key. This shuts down tool-misuse at the protocol level."

[02:00 – 02:45] BUSINESS IMPACT & PERFORMANCE
"The empirical results are clear. Across 1,600 adversarial prompts, our system maintained a 95.4% accuracy rate. We’ve seen the Attack Success Rate drop from 80% to under 5%. 

And the best part? Performance overhead is minimal. Our regex filter blocks obvious attacks in under 5 milliseconds. We’ve built this to run locally—ensuring your sensitive data never leaves your infrastructure."

[02:45 – 03:00] THE TRANSITION

[03:30 – 04:30] ADAPTIVE LAYERS: L3, L4, & L5
"This brings us to Layer 3: our proprietary Agent Stability Index, or ASI. In a multi-turn conversation, an attacker might spend 10 turns slowly steering an agent. We track semantic drift and consistency over time. If the ASI score drops below our threshold, the session is quarantined instantly.

Layer 4 is our autonomous Red Teaming engine. We use a PPO reinforcement learning agent that continuously tries to bypass our own gateway. It finds weaknesses, evaluates them, and proposes policy updates—automatically. 

And finally, Layer 5 is Output Mediation. Even if the input is clean, we scan the LLM's response for PII like SSNs or API keys and redact them in real-time. It’s zero-trust at every exit."

[04:30 – 05:15] MULTI-AGENT ORCHESTRATION
"Beyond security, we’ve implemented a sophisticated Multi-Agent Router. Whether it’s an HR Database agent with PII masking, a sandboxed Code Execution agent, or a Financial Advisor, our system routes triggers to the right specialist.

Each of these agents operates under a scoped permission set. The email agent can't touch the database, and the HR agent can't execute code. It’s a principle of least privilege, enforced not just by prompts, but by our security gateway."
